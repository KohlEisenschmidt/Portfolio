About Me

I am a Full Stack Developer who enjoys challenges that allow me to build and design real-world applications that equip users with the tools they need to succeed. I hold a bachelor's degree in Philosophy and have current training from the UNC Charlotte Full Stack web development program. My background in creating and fabricating, coupled with my innate passion for learning, laid the groundwork for furthering my technical expertise by returning to school.

I am proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery, Node.js, Command Line, GIT, Github, various style frameworks including Bootstrap and Materialize. I additionally have experience in Express, React, Handlebars and have worked with databases such as Firebase, MySQL with Sequelize and MongoDB with Mongoose
